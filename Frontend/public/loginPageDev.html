SAIP Image Archive


Click the "NIH Login" button, and you will be redirected to the NIH login page for AD authentication

You will enter the SAIP Image Archive website after successful Login.





Basic Usage:
  • Select time period (last one week by default) and then clickto search
  • In the "Study" table, click the at the end of each row to check details for study
  • Click the download will download zipped package for the selected study or patient/series
